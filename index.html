About Us

Discover the story behind Green Soul and our mission to bring people closer to nature

Welcome to Mount Machi Resort

Green Soul was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to create spaces where people can reconnect with nature without sacrificing comfort. Our brand believes in sustainable tourism that respects the environment while providing unforgettable experiences.

Mount Machi Resort is our flagship property, designed specifically for families, couples, and groups looking for a peaceful getaway surrounded by lush greenery. Located in a serene location, it offers the perfect weekend escape from city life.

At Mount Machi, we've carefully crafted every aspect of your stay to ensure you leave feeling refreshed, rejuvenated, and more connected to the natural world around you.
